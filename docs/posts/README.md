## Kaise use karein

1. **Admin → New Post** kholo.
2. Har file ke andar **Meta table** se **Title**, **Slug**, **Excerpt** copy karo aur form me daalo.
3. **Content** section (markdown) ko copy karo aur **Content** field me paste karo.
4. **Category** select karo (e.g. Exam Preparation, Study Tips).
5. **Featured image** optional – agar ho to upload karo.
6. **Publish** karo.
7. Agar app bolta hai "Post must be at least 1500 words", to content me 1–2 short paragraphs add karke dubara submit karo.

## SEO / Indexing

- Har post me **slug**, **excerpt**, **title** SEO-friendly diye hain.
- Publish ke baad **category** zaroor assign karo taaki internal links strong rahein.
- GSC me **URL Inspection** se **Request indexing** kar sakte ho.
